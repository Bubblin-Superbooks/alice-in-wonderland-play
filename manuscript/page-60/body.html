Ah! Then yours wasn’t a really good school. Now at ours they had at the end of the bill, French, music, _and washing_—extra.

Alice

You couldn’t have wanted it much; living at the bottom of the sea.

Mock Turtle

I couldn’t afford to learn it, I only took the regular course.

Alice

What was that?

Mock Turtle

Reeling and writhing, of course, to begin with, and then the different branches of Arithmetic—Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, and Derision.

Alice

I never heard of Uglification. What is it?
