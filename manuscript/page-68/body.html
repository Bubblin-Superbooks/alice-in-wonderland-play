[The creatures dance against Alice, _pushing her back and forth between them. She protests and finally escapes; they bump against each other._]

Alice

Thank you; it’s a very interesting dance to watch, and I do so like that curious song about the whiting.

Mock Turtle

Oh, as to the whiting, they—you’ve seen them, of course?
