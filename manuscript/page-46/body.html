Alice

I’m sorry—you dropped them, you know—

White Rabbit

[_Picks up fan and gloves and patters off._]

She’ll chop off your head!

Alice

If you please sir—where am I?—won’t you please—tell me how to get out—I want to get out—

White Rabbit

[_Looking at his watch._]

Oh! my ears and whiskers, how late it’s getting.

[A trap door gives way and Rabbit disappears. Alice _dashes after only in time to have the trap door bang in her face._]

Alice

[_Amazed._]

It’s a rabbit-hole—I’m small enough to fit it too! If I shrink any more it might end in my going out
