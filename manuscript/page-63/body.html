That’s the reason they’re called lessons, because they lessen from day to day.

Alice

Then the eleventh day must have been a holiday?

Mock Turtle

Of course it was.

Alice

And how did you manage on the twelfth?

Gryphon

That’s enough about lessons, tell her something about the games now.

[Mock Turtle sighs deeply, draws back of one flapper across his eyes. He looks at Alice _and tries to speak but sobs choke his voice._]

Gryphon

[_Punching him in the back._]

Same as if he had a bone in his throat.

Mock Turtle
