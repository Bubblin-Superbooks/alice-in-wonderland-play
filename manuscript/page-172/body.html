[_Fumbles with the parchment, then cries in a shrill little voice._]

Alice!

Alice

Here!

King

What do you know about this business?

Alice

Nothing whatever.

King

[_To the jury._]

That’s very important.

White Rabbit

Un_important, your Majesty means, of course.

King

Un_important, of course I meant. Important—unimportant—unimportant—important. Consider your verdict!
