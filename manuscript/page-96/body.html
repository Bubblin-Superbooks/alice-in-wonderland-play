March Hare

Let’s move on.

[The platform moves off with table, chairs, March Hare, Hatter, and Dormouse. Meanwhile the Frog has come out again and is sitting near the closed door, staring stupidly at the sky. Alice _goes to the door timidly and knocks._]

Frog

There’s no sort of use in knocking, and that for two reasons: first, because I’m on the same side of the door as you are; secondly, because they’re making such a noise inside, no one could possibly hear you.

Alice

Please then, how am I to get in?

Frog

There might be some sense in your knocking if we had the door between us. For instance, if you were inside, you might knock, and I could let you out, you
