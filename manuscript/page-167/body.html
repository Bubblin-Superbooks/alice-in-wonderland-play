King

The twinkling of what?

Hatter

It began with the tea.

King

Of course twinkling begins with a T. Do you take me for a dunce? Go on!

Hatter

I’m a poor man and most things twinkled after that—only the March Hare said—

March Hare

I didn’t!

Hatter

You did.
