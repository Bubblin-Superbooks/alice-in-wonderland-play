Knave offers himself as an arch for Alice. Even though Alice does not notice him he holds the arch position. The Queen _shouts at intervals, “Off with his head, off with her head.”_]

Alice

Where are the Chess Queens?

Rabbit

Under sentence of execution.

Alice

What for?

Rabbit

Did you say, “what a pity”?

Alice

No, I didn’t. I don’t think it’s at all a pity. I said, “What for?”

Rabbit

They boxed the Queen’s ears.

[Alice _gives a little scream of laughter._]
