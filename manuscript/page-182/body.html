Scene Three

[The curtain rises to show Alice _still asleep in the armchair, the fire in the grate suffusing her with its glow._]

Carroll

Wake up, Alice, it is time for tea.

[Off stage the characters repeat their most characteristic lines, “Off with her head,” “Consider your verdict,” “Oh! my fur and whiskers”; the Duchess sneezes, the cat cries, as if the characters were fading away into the pack of real playing cards which shower through the mirror all over Alice. _There is music._]

Alice
